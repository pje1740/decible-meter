About Accuracy

This decibel meter provides approximate measurements and may not be as accurate as professional sound level meters. The readings can be affected by:

  • Microphone quality and sensitivity
  • Background noise and room acoustics
  • Device hardware and software limitations

For more accurate measurements:

  • Use an external, calibrated microphone
  • Measure in a controlled environment
  • Compare readings with a professional sound level meter

This tool is for informational purposes only and should not be used for professional or regulatory compliance measurements.
